AKAN NAME GENERATOR

This site will take your birth date, calculate the day of the week you were born and depending on your gender (male/female) it outputs your Akan name: ( The first name given to children in Ghanian culture according to the day of the week they were born.) For more information Click here. Kindly choose your birthday and gender from the form at the bottom left and submit to see your Akan name.
