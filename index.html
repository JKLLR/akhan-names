AKAN NAME GENERATOR

this site will take your birthday date, calculate the day of the week you were born and depending on your gender(male/female) it outputs your Akan name(Akan-The first name given to children in Ghanian culture according to the day of the week they were born.)

Day Male Female
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yow Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
